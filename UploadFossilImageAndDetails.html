Image Upload Guidelines

Please upload image files only in JPEG, JPG, PNG, GIF formats.

A Field Number visible on at least one image of the specimen is required.

Each image file size must be below 250 KB.

To reduce image size, use reduceimages.com — a free online tool.

Maximum 4 images per specimen.

After uploading your images, immediately fill in the Fossil Details Form.
