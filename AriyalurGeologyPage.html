Introduction to a Fossil Sites in Ariyalur

The Cretaceous rocks are exposed in Ariyalur area in South India.
These rocks formations are grouped into four namely, the Niniyur, Ariyalur, Trichinopoly, and Uttatur, groups.

Outcrops of these groups are seen in Ariyalur and in its surounding areas.
In these outcrops we find fossils like Ammonites, Bivalues, Gastropods, Echinoids, Plant fossils, etc.

This area was and is continuously visited and studied by many eminent geologists.
Many foreign geologist have come here and studied/explored the geology of this area.
For students studying geology, this area is a must-visit.
Many colleges are conducting study tours to impart field experience for their students.
